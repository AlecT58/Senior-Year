      The days of the week in sequence are:
               1. Monday
               2. Tuesday
               3. Wednesday
               4. Thursday
               5. Friday
               6. Saturday

  * The month of their birth
  * The day of their birth
  * The year of their birth

* Display in tabular format the calendar for that particular month in which the person was born
